GENERAL OBJ - get to know the association operators and see what they are for

Route - stage 01 - getting to know the association operators

WHAT ARE ?
These are operators used to check whether an object is present in a sequence

Example
course = "Python Course"
fruit = ["orange", "grape", "lemon"]
withdrawals = [1500, 100]

"Python" in course
True

"apple" not in fruit
True

200 in withdrawals
false
